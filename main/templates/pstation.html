Surat city is situated on the western side of India in the state of Gujarat. Surat is also a very clean city and there are several names that have been given to the city such as “The Diamond City”, “The Silk City” and “The Green City”. Surat is a city where the Britishers first landed in India. Surat also served as a port in the past where the boats of more than 84 countries used to harbor. Surat Police is divided into four Zones i.e Zone -I, Zone -II, Zone -III & Zone- IV each headed by Deputy Commisoner of Police. These Four zones are further divided into 8 Divisons.
